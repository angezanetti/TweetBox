TweetBox is a simple project made by @AngeZanetti & @Guitool of the Coworking space of Lille.

It's a simple Arduino board, fixed on a box. With 1 buttons & 1 potentiometer. 

Select your statut with the potentiometer, push the button to tweet.

You can now update, deleted or get the status with a simple HTTP request.

Have fun with it !
---- 

You need a Node.js serveur with expresse & tweasy to make it works.
Both can be installed on your serveur with npm - http://npmjs.org/.

Have fun !
